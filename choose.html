Choose your hero

Choose one of these three heroes. Base your decisions on what you think that hero would do.

  • Warrior

    A Warrior is physically strong but not very smart. Your choices should reflect on brute strength .

  • ROGUE

    A rogue is not physically very strong but is very althletic and quick. Your choices should reflect on speed, agility.

  • WIZARD

    A wizard is not physically strong nor athletic but is very inteligent. You must use your mind and cunning to survive as well as a few spells.
