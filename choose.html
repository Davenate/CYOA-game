Choose your hero

Choose one of these three heroes. Base your decisions on what you think that hero would do.

  • Warrior

    A Warrior is physically strong but not very smart. Your choices should reflect on brute strength .

  • Rogue

    A rogue is not physically very strong but is very althletic and quick. Your choices should reflect on speed, agility.

  • Apprentice

    The apprentice wizard is not physically strong nor athletic but is very inteligent. You must use your mind and cunning to survive as well as a few spells.

The Tavern

Your journey begins like most, inside a tavern. You overhear two men from another table taking about a cave deep in the forest that holds a wonderous treasure. Of course, many have entered but none have ever returned. Well, treasure is great but wonderous treasure? That sounds like something to risk your life over! Suddenly on the other side of the tavern you hear someone yell and a see a table get flipped. A brawl errupts in the far end of the tavern. What will you do?
